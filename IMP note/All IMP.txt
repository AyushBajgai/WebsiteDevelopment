Navbar concept

navbar-light to set a theme
bg-light for background theme
navbar-nav means wraps the navbar items such as Home,Menu,Contact and so on.
navbar-toggler-icon means the number of togglers required to display menu items.
navbar-toggler toggles the menu items for ml or sm screen
data-toggle is used by JS when clicking on it, it displays the items.
data-target means the items that we want to toggle (given by id)
